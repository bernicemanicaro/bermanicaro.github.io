Hi, my name is Bernice Manicaro. I am currently in my third year in Bachelor of Fine Arts Honours in Digital Arts at the University of Malta. Throughout these past three years we have covered traditional and digital illustration, photography, editorial and packaging design, branding, comic art and many others.

Art and creativity have always been a part of my life since I was a little child. After long and boring hours of homework I used to find myself holding a pencil or a paintbrush against a piece of paper. I started studying art at the age of 12 in secondary school and continued my studies through 6th form with my Art A-level.

My mission now is to grow my portfolio as well as to find myself as an artist.
